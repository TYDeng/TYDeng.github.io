About Us

Welcome to Shadow BBQ Smokehouse, where the rich flavors of traditional BBQ meet the warmth of Southern hospitality. Our story is one of passion, community, and a relentless pursuit of perfection in every dish we serve.

Our Mission

At Shadow BBQ Smokehouse, our mission is simple: to create unforgettable BBQ experiences that bring people together. We believe in the power of good food to foster connections, celebrate moments, and create lasting memories. Every meal we serve is a testament to our dedication to quality, authenticity, and community.


Our Philosophy

Quality Ingredients: We source the finest cuts of meat and freshest ingredients to ensure every bite is bursting with flavor. Our commitment to quality begins with selecting the best raw materials and ends with a perfectly smoked masterpiece.

Traditional Techniques: We honor the time-honored traditions of BBQ, using slow-cooking methods and carefully curated wood blends to achieve the perfect smoke. Our pitmasters are artisans, dedicating hours to perfect each batch, ensuring that every piece of meat is tender, juicy, and packed with smoky goodness.

Our Offerings

Signature BBQ: From succulent ribs and pulled pork to mouth-watering brisket and chicken, our menu is a celebration of BBQ done right. Each dish is crafted with care, seasoned with our signature rubs, and smoked to perfection.

Homemade Sides: Complement your BBQ experience with our selection of homemade sides. From creamy coleslaw and buttery cornbread to tangy baked beans and savory mac ‘n’ cheese, our sides are made with the same love and attention as our main dishes.

Special Events: Whether you’re planning a family gathering, a corporate event, or a community celebration, Shadow BBQ Smokehouse offers catering services that bring our delicious BBQ right to your doorstep. Let us make your next event unforgettable with our mouth-watering offerings.


Our Team

Our team is the backbone of Shadow BBQ Smokehouse. From our skilled pitmasters to our friendly front-of-house staff, each member of our team shares a passion for BBQ and a dedication to providing exceptional service. We are a family, united by our love for great food and our commitment to creating a welcoming environment for all.
